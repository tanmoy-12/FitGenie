Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay for my subscription?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards and online payment gateways. You can choose the payment method that is most convenient for you during the checkout process.

Can I cancel or reschedule my training session?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your training session by contacting our customer support team or using our online session management system. Please make sure to notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.

Do you have subscription plans?

Yes, we offer a variety of subscription plans to suit your needs. Please refer to our subscription plans section for detailed information or contact our support team for assistance.

What fitness services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of fitness services including personalized training programs, nutrition guidance, virtual classes, and progress tracking. Please refer to our Services section for more details.

How do I book a training session?

To book a training session, simply click on the "Book Session" button on our homepage. You will be directed to our booking system where you can choose your preferred date and time.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take the security of your personal information very seriously. Our platform uses the latest encryption technology to ensure that your data is protected at all times.
